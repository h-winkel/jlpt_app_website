Benkyo Buddy

Your Japanese learning companion

About Benkyo Buddy

Benkyo Buddy is a comprehensive Japanese learning app designed for steady, daily practice. It is based on real experience learning Japanese over many years — not on short-term hacks. The goal is not to be the fastest method, but to provide all the tools needed to actually maintain long-term progress, every day.

Spaced-Repetition Vocabulary Practice

Benkyo Buddy includes SRS flashcards for over 9,000 words, ordered by JLPT level and part of speech. Each entry comes with example sentences and audio generated with Google’s current Text-to-Speech engine.

Kanji Drawing & Mnemonics

For every vocabulary item, users can draw each kanji directly in the app, and optionally write their own mnemonic story for each character. Built-in kanji and radical lists show meanings at a glance.

Grammar Practice & Reference

The app includes over 700 grammar points, which unlock as you learn relevant vocabulary. Six exercise types help internalize each grammar point:

・Starred blanks (insert the missing word)
・Sentence word ordering
・Conjugation exercises
・Translation (JP ↔ EN)
・Particle fill-in
・Verb transitivity identification

Every grammar point also doubles as a reference entry you can browse.

Listening Practice

The app plays a Japanese sentence and you reproduce it by ordering the words correctly.

Built-In “Ask the Developer” Forum

A small forum is included where users can give feedback, request features, and ask questions about the app’s direction.

Use Across Multiple Devices

Your progress can be synced between devices — learn anywhere.

Search Vocabulary, Grammar, Kanji

A unified search screen allows you to look up any entry you’ve learned (or reset entries you’d like to relearn).

Free N5 Tier (No Registration Required)

Try the app from JLPT N5 entirely offline — no account needed. (N5 Free Mode does not include the forum, settings, or statistics screens.)
